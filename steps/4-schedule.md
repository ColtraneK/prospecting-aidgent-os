# Step 4 — Schedule it

Goal: the same sourcing job, every weekday, without you starting it. This is the difference between a chatbot and an agent: it does the work while you sleep, and you wake up to fresh rows to review.

## Do it (ChatGPT)

1. Get the manual run working first (Step 3, Method A). Scheduling only makes sense once the output is good.
2. Open the scheduler at [chatgpt.com/schedules](https://chatgpt.com/schedules), or click the Clock icon in the chat.
3. Paste prompt 4 from [PROMPTS.md](../PROMPTS.md), set it to repeat every weekday at your time.

## What it does

Each weekday it appends fresh prospects to the Leads tab. Fresh means the name and profile URL are not already in the sheet, so it adds new rows without touching your human tracking. It sources from the public web, no sign-ins, so it runs with your computer off.

## Keep it honest

The schedule only sources and drafts. It never messages, connects, comments, or posts. You still open the sheet, review, and reach out yourself. Measure real conversations, not row counts.

## Your daily loop

Open Leads. For each new person: read Why Them, open the source link, use or edit the Suggested Opener, reach out yourself, then track reality in the G to M columns (Reached Out, Replied, Outcome).
